SSFabtech Products

Engineered Excellence in uPVC Doors & Windows

At SSFABTECH, we bring over 15 years of hands-on expertise in uPVC window and door fabrication. We've now expanded into the next phase—manufacturing high-performance uPVC profiles to set new standards in quality, innovation, and sustainability.

Our profiles are sourced from Penta Extrusions India Pvt. Ltd, a trusted part of our group company, ensuring superior consistency and strength in every frame we fabricate.

  • ✔️ Multi-chambered profile design for top-notch insulation and energy efficiency
  • ✔️ Weatherproof, salt-resistant — ideal for coastal and tropical climates
  • ✔️ Precision fusion-welded joints for strength and leak-proof durability
  • ✔️ Wide variety of styles: sliding, casement, tilt & turn, arch, and more
  • ✔️ Excellent acoustic insulation when combined with laminated or double glazing
  • ✔️ Eco-friendly, termite-resistant, and virtually maintenance-free

Our Premium Products

Explore our range of high-quality window solutions

Bay Window

A type of window structure that protrudes outward from the main walls of a building, forming a bay inside the room.

Combination Window

A combination window is a mix of two or more different types of windows fixed + sliding, casement + fixed within a single frame.

Casement Window

A casement window is a side-hinged window that opens outward like a door, usually operated with a crank or handle.

Casement Door

A casement door is a side-hinged door that opens like a regular door, often with glass panels.

Combination Door

Combination doors offer the perfect mix of style, function, and natural light entry.

Sliding Door

Space-saving sliding doors that glide effortlessly and bring your outdoors indoors.

Windows with grills

uPVC windows with grills combine durable uPVC frames and protective or decorative metal grills.

uPVC Ventilators

Small window units designed to provide airflow in areas like bathrooms, kitchens, or staircases.
