About SSfabtech

Creating beautiful, energy-efficient homes since 2010

At SSFABTECH, we bring over 15 years of hands-on experience in uPVC window fabrication, and we're proud to step into the next level of excellence with the manufacturing of high-quality uPVC profiles. What began as a focused effort in fabrication has evolved into a forward-thinking journey rooted in quality, sustainability, and innovation.

uPVC (unplasticized polyvinyl chloride) is more than just a building material—it represents our commitment to creating structures that are visually striking and built to perform. With uPVC, we support eco-conscious choices by reducing reliance on deforestation, one window and door at a time.

  • Producing LEAD-FREE uPVC profiles that are safe and recyclable.
  • Sourcing raw materials from Penta Extrusions India Pvt. Ltd, India to ensure global-grade quality.
  • Constantly innovating to meet both environmental standards and customer satisfaction.

Key Features

Experience premium quality windows backed by exceptional service

DUST PROOFING & AIR TIGHTNESS

All joints of uPVC windows are sealed by fusion welding & gaps are filled with silicon hence making it air tight & dust proof.

IMPACT RESISTANCE

Manufactured with special impact -resistance formula, Plastech uPVC Window profile can bear cold impact test of 1kg hammer dropping freely from one meter height at -10°C .

SOUND INSULATION

Multiple chamber profile design with seamless junction gives very good sound insulation effect. Compared with metal materials, Plastech uPVC can reduce 20 to 30db noise.

WATERPROOF FUNCTION

Water absorption on uPVC profiles is below 0.1%. It is equipped with rainproof plates and water drain slots hence the window and door will completely isolate rain water outside of your room.

ENVIRONMENT PROTECTION

Regarded as fourth new construction material, the Plastech uPVC Window has apparent environmental protection and energy saving character, which is recommended for broad use and development.
