Sliding Doors

Engineered Excellence in uPVC Doors & Windows

Sliding doors operate by moving horizontally along tracks, making them ideal for areas with limited space. They offer a sleek, modern look while allowing maximum natural light and a clear view of the outdoors. Crafted from high-quality uPVC profiles by Penta Extrusions India Pvt. Ltd., our sliding doors are built for durability, smooth operation, and lasting performance. Our profiles are sourced from Penta Extrusions India Pvt. Ltd, a trusted part of our group company, ensuring superior consistency and strength in every frame we fabricate.

    Advantages of Sliding Doors


    • Space-saving design with smooth sliding operation
    • Allows abundant natural light and outdoor views
    • Ideal for balconies, patios, and large openings
    • Low-maintenance and resistant to rust and corrosion
    • Energy-efficient with sound insulation options
    • Customizable panel sizes, glass types, and finishes
