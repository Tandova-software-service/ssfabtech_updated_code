About SSFabtech

Creating beautiful, energy-efficient homes since 2010

At SSFABTECH, we bring over 15 years of hands-on experience in uPVC window fabrication, and we're proud to step into the next level of excellence with the manufacturing of high-quality uPVC profiles. What began as a focused effort in fabrication has evolved into a forward-thinking journey rooted in quality, sustainability, and innovation.

uPVC (unplasticized polyvinyl chloride) is more than just a building material—it represents our commitment to creating structures that are visually striking and built to perform. With uPVC, we support eco-conscious choices by reducing reliance on deforestation, one window and door at a time.

  • Producing LEAD-FREE uPVC profiles that are safe and recyclable.
  • Sourcing raw materials from Penta Extrusions, India to ensure global-grade quality.
  • Constantly innovating to meet both environmental standards and customer satisfaction.
